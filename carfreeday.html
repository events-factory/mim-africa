Kigali Car Free Day: Africa Walks Against Malaria

As part of the MIM Society 8th Pan-African Malaria Conference and in commemoration of World Malaria Day, we are organizing a special event: “Africa Walks Against Malaria”, with the aim of raising awareness about malaria prevention, treatment, and research across the African continent.

Date: April 21, 2024

Itinerary: Starting from BK Arena in Remera, participants will walk to the Kigali Heights Roundabout.

Route map: Click here

Time: 7:00 am to 10:00 am

What to Wear: Please wear a comfortable walking outfit and running shoes.

Bus Pick-Up and Drop-Off: For conference participants, there will be a bus pick-up available from 6 AM at your respective hotels, transporting you to the BK Arena where the walk will commence. After the walk, buses will take you back from the finish line to your hotels.

Join us in this collective effort to combat malaria and create a healthier Africa.

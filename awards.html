The MIM Society Award: Celebrating Excellence

The MIM Society recognizes the outstanding contributions of malaria researchers and individuals who have played a pivotal role in the global fight against malaria. Their unwavering dedication and expertise are critical in combatting this deadly disease worldwide.

The MIM Society Awards are established in honor of eminent malaria scientists, researchers, and public health advocates: Prof. Ogobara Doumbo, Dr. Brian Sharp, and Dr. Peter J. de Vries.

Spread the Word!

The Multilateral Initiative on Malaria (MIM Society) 8th Pan-African Malaria Conference is coming to Kigali, Rwanda from 21-27 April 2024.

We have added social media content that will you stay informed about the conference and provide you with the necessary resources to promote the conference on your social media platforms. Please feel free to download the graphics provided on this page and share them on your social media platforms to spread the word about this important event.

The toolkit will be updated with additional content and social media assets closer to the conference.

If you have questions or suggestions, please email: mim.queries@rbc.gov.rw

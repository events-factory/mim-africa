Sponsorship Opportunities

MIM SOCIETY 2024 Sponsorship packages will include a range of opportunities for acknowledgement and visibility, including exhibition spaces, and sponsored symposiums, and delegate registrations in addition to numerous branding opportunities. The Conference offers prime recognition for sponsors who support ongoing MIM SOCIETY programming in addition to the conference.

Several levels of sponsorships are available and each tier provides sponsors with the ability to leverage the brand strength of MIM SOCIETY while you network and promote your brand and image.

Announcements & News

Press Release

Kigali, April 21, 2024 - The Multilateral Initiative on Malaria (MIM) Society, in collaboration with the Rwanda Ministry of Health and the Rwanda Biomedical Center is pleased to announce the commencement of the 8th Pan-African Malaria Conference. This pivotal gathering will bring together global experts, researchers, policy makers, and innovators, and will take place from April 21-27, 2024, in Kigali, Rwanda.

Media Advisory

For Foreign Media: Foreign media professionals, traveling to Rwanda for media purposes must obtain media accreditation. To apply, visit the IremboGov website and select the appropriate fields under the media category.

For Symposium Organizers: If you are organizing a symposium at the 8th Pan African Malaria Conference and would like to distribute materials about your organization or symposium during the scheduled official press conference events, please reach out to the communication team at mim.queries@rbc.gov.rw

RWANDA TO HOST THE 8TH PAN-AFRICAN MALARIA CONFERENCE ON WORLD MALARIA DAY (WMD) 2024

Rwanda will host the Multilateral Initiative on Malaria (MIM Society) 8th Pan-African Malaria Conference (PAMC) from 21-27 April 2024 under the theme, ‘Grassroots Mobilization to End Malaria: Invest, Innovate & Integrate’.

Celebrate the WMD 2024 with the 8th PAMC

The much-anticipated Multilateral Initiative on Malaria Society (MIM Society) 8th Pan-African Malaria Conference, is set to take place this year in Rwanda. This celebration of achievements during the World Malaria Day (WMD) falls within the conference days of April 21-27, 2024.

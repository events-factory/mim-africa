For Foreign Media:Foreign media professionals, traveling to Rwanda for media purposes must obtain media accreditation. To apply, visit the IremboGov website and select the appropriate fields under the media category. It is crucial for all reporters and film crew members to declare the list of media equipment they are carrying to avoid import duties.

For Symposium Organizers: If you are organizing a symposium at the 8th Pan African Malaria Conference and would like to distribute materials about your organization or symposium during the scheduled official press conference events, please reach out to the communication team at mim.queries@rbc.gov.rw
